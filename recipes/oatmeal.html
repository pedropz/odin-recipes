Microwave Oatmeal

Description

A quick and healthy option for one of those colder days. It requires very little planning and is adaptable to as many or as little
ingredients as you want to add or have available.

Ingredients

  • oats
  • any type of milk
  • bananas
  • any berries

Optionally:

  • peanut butter
  • vanilla

Steps

  1. First, pour some oats and milk in a bowl. Preferably in a 1-to-1 ratio, but it's up to personal choice. I also recommend you pour
    the oats first, as if you use the same measuring cup for the milk first, some of the oats might stick to it.

    2. This is where you can drop a teaspoon of vanilla, if you choose to do so.

  2. Stir the bowl.
  3. Put your bowl in the microwave for around 90 seconds. This might vary depending on the model of your microwave.
  4. Now, put whatever fruits you want on top of your oatmeal - and peanut butter if you want.
  5. Enjoy!
